Our Story

Phoenix Air Services, Inc. is a well-established, family owned and operated industrial and commercial HVAC service company. Phoenix Air Services was founded in 1990 by Bernie Beauchemin, and for the last 10 years, his son, Bob Beauchemin has made it his life's work to provide outstanding and professional service work to Phoenix Air's many long-term clients. Phoenix Air Services is able to provide superior service work to all scopes of industrial and commercial businesses because we have reliable and trustworthy suppliers and subcontractors we have partnered with over these last 30 years.
